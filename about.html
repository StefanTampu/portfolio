About Me

Since graduating high school in 2015, I have studied history at university, taught math in middle & high school, and am now looking to expand my jack-of-all-trades repertoire by launching into web development. I have worked through courses on HTML, CSS and JavaScript and personal projects for the web since 2022, and I am now expanding my skills in SEO and UX/UI.

Vision: To build websites that are both simple and beautiful, working to support your business and draw in customers.

Mission: To work with you to deliver websites that streamline your business - not complicate it!

Often a website can be just an extra expense for a business. Instead, I wish to consult closely with you to understand what your problems and needs are. The goal is to create a site that takes a load off your hands, and brings in the customers you want. In other words, let's talk, and see how a web site can be a helpful tool for you!
